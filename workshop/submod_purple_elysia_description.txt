[h1]Summary[/h1]

Elysian map and flag color customization mod for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=387726157]Third Odyssey: Back to the Motherland[/url]


[h1]Compatibility[/h1]

Mod version 1.0.4 - compatible with Third Odyssey 1.23.X (EU4 1.36.*)

[b]Warning[/b]: This mod MUST be enabled before starting a new game! Nation colors are saved in savegames for some reason... You can attempt to edit your savegame though.
