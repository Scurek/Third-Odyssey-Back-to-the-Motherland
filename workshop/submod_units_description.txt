[h1]Summary[/h1]

Units mod for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=387726157]Third Odyssey: Back to the Motherland[/url]


[hr][/hr]

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
Mod version 1.0.0 - compatible with Third Odyssey 1.30.0 (EU4 1.33.2.*)
Requires "The Cossacks Content Pack" DLC


[h1]Content[/h1]

Custom unit models built on top of vanilla (The Cossacks Content Pack) models will be enabled for Elysia.
If you do not use this submod, the base mod will use other (free) vanilla models as a base.
